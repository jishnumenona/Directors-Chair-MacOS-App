# Monetization Strategy — DirectorsChair

**Date**: March 2026

---

## Pricing Philosophy

DirectorsChair should follow the **"freemium with AI metering"** model:
- **Free tier** to eliminate adoption barriers and build user base
- **Paid tiers** unlock AI features, cloud sync, and collaboration
- **AI usage metered** to align cost with value delivered

This approach works because:
1. The tool has high upfront complexity — users need time to discover value
2. AI features have real per-use cost (API calls) that must be recouped
3. Indie filmmakers are price-sensitive but will pay for tools that save time
4. Film schools need institutional pricing with volume discounts

## Revenue Model Breakdown

### Subscription Revenue (Primary — 70% of revenue)
Recurring monthly/annual subscriptions are the foundation. Annual plans at 17% discount incentivize commitment and improve cash flow predictability.

### AI Credit Top-ups (Secondary — 20% of revenue)
Users who exceed tier limits can purchase additional AI credits:

| Credit Pack | Price | Approximate Value |
|-------------|-------|-------------------|
| 100 text generations | $4.99 | ~$0.05/generation |
| 50 image generations | $9.99 | ~$0.20/generation |
| 10 video generations | $14.99 | ~$1.50/generation |
| Mixed pack (50 text + 20 image + 5 video) | $14.99 | Best value |

**Margin analysis**: Average AI API cost per generation:
- Text (Gemini): ~$0.005-0.02 per call → 75-90% margin
- Image (Imagen): ~$0.04-0.08 per image → 60-80% margin
- Video (Veo): ~$0.50-1.00 per video → 30-50% margin
- Speech (ElevenLabs): ~$0.01-0.05 per generation → 70-85% margin

### Assembly Pipeline Value Justification
The Automated Footage Assembly Pipeline dramatically changes the value proposition:
- **Indie short film**: Saves 2-5 days of manual assembly work (valued at $500-2,000)
- **Indie feature**: Saves 2-4 weeks of assistant editor work (valued at $2,000-8,000)
- **Commercial production**: Saves 1-3 days per project, dozens of projects/year (valued at $10,000-30,000/year)
- **Studio production**: Compresses post-production timeline by 1-3 weeks (valued at $20,000-100,000+)

At $39.99/month ($480/year), the Studio tier pays for itself on a **single short film**. This is not a cost — it's an investment with immediate, measurable ROI. This value proposition is unique among all filmmaking tools.

### Marketplace Commission (Future — 10% of revenue)
30% commission on user-generated content sales:
- Screenplay templates and structures
- Character archetype packs
- LUT presets and film styles
- Shot list templates
- Budget templates
- Location reference packs

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Private Beta (Month 1-2)
- Invite 100-200 filmmakers from indie communities
- Free access to all features
- Collect feedback, identify bugs, validate pricing

### Phase 2: Public Beta with Freemium (Month 3-4)
- Launch Free + Pro tiers
- "Founding Member" discount: $9.99/mo for first 500 Pro subscribers (locked for 12 months)
- Gather testimonials and case studies

### Phase 3: Full Launch (Month 5-6)
- Add Studio and Education tiers
- Launch on Product Hunt, Hacker News, filmmaking communities
- Begin film school outreach program

### Phase 4: Marketplace & Expansion (Month 9-12)
- Launch template marketplace
- Introduce AI credit top-up packs
- Begin enterprise conversations
